## üìù AI Title Generation

### How It Works

Based on your article content, AI analyzes the theme and core points to automatically generate 10 titles in different styles.

### Usage Steps

1. **Open AI Writing Panel**
   - Click left sidebar
   - Switch to "AI Writing" tab

2. **Input Article Content**
   - Paste or type article text
   - Recommend at least 200 words for better results

3. **Generate Titles**
   - Click "Generate Titles" button
   - Wait for AI processing (usually 5-10 seconds)
   - View 10 generated titles

4. **Use Titles**
   - Click title to copy to clipboard
   - Or click "Insert to Editor" to insert directly

### Title Types

AI generates various title types:

#### 1. Emotional Resonance
Evoke reader emotions through emotional words.

**Examples:**
- "5 Life Lessons Everyone Should Know"
- "Why Does Hard Work Lead to Anxiety?"

#### 2. Suspense & Curiosity
Create suspense to spark curiosity.

**Examples:**
- "This Method Made Me $10K/Month, But I Never Tell Anyone"
- "90% of People Don't Know This Secret"

#### 3. Practical Value
Emphasize practical value and useful content.

**Examples:**
- "Complete Python Beginner's Guide (2025 Edition)"
- "10 Tools to Boost Your Productivity"

#### 4. Numbered Lists
Use numbers to enhance appeal.

**Examples:**
- "7 Skills That Make You Stand Out"
- "3 Steps to Master Investment"

#### 5. Contrast & Comparison
Create impact through contrast.

**Examples:**
- "From $3K/Month to $50K/Year, What Did I Do Right?"
- "Same Writing, Why Can They Make Money?"

### Optimization Tips

**Input Content Guidelines:**
- ‚úÖ Complete content with clear logic
- ‚úÖ Include core points and arguments
- ‚úÖ Sufficient word count (recommend 500+ words)
- ‚ùå Avoid fragmented content
- ‚ùå Avoid overly brief descriptions

**Improve Results:**
1. Provide core article points

3. Indicate desired title style

## üìä AI Title Scoring

### How It Works

Score existing titles from 0-100 with detailed analysis and optimization suggestions.

### Usage Steps

1. **Input Title**
   - Enter title to score
   - Can be your own or AI-generated

2. **Get Score**
   - Click "Score" button
   - View overall score (0-100)

3. **View Analysis**
   - Read detailed dimension analysis
   - Check optimization suggestions

4. **Optimize Title**
   - Modify based on suggestions
   - Re-score to verify improvement

### Scoring Dimensions

Title scoring includes 5 dimensions:

#### 1. Attractiveness (30 points)
- Catches reader attention
- Creates click desire
- Emotional word usage

#### 2. Clarity (25 points)
- Clear topic
- Clear expression
- Easy to understand

#### 3. Emotional Intensity (20 points)
- Adequate emotional expression
- Evokes resonance
- Appropriate tone

#### 4. Keyword Popularity (15 points)
- Includes trending keywords
- Keyword search volume
- SEO friendly

#### 5. Appropriate Length (10 points)
- Suitable title length
- Recommend 15-30 characters
- Mobile display effect

### Scoring Examples

**High Score Title (85 points):**
> "From $3K/Month to $50K/Year: 3 Methods I Used"

**Analysis:**
- ‚úÖ Strong appeal (contrast)
- ‚úÖ Clear topic (career development)
- ‚úÖ Specific numbers (3 methods)
- ‚úÖ Appropriate length (22 chars)

**Low Score Title (45 points):**
> "Some Thoughts"

**Analysis:**
- ‚ùå Weak appeal (too vague)
- ‚ùå Unclear topic
- ‚ùå Lacks emotional expression
- ‚ùå Too brief

### How It Works

Input a topic or keywords, and AI automatically generates a complete article outline or full text.

### Usage Steps

1. **Input Topic**
   - Describe article topic
   - Can include keywords, target audience, article style

2. **Choose Generation Type**
   - **Outline Mode** - Generate article structure and key points
   - **Full Text Mode** - Generate complete article

3. **Set Parameters** (Optional)
   - Article length (short/medium/long)
   - Writing style (formal/casual/professional)
   - Target audience (beginner/intermediate/expert)

4. **Generate Content**
   - Click "Generate Article" button
   - Wait for AI processing
   - View generated result

5. **Edit and Use**
   - Review content in preview area
   - Manually edit as needed
   - Click "Insert to Editor" to use

## üé® AI Text Rewriting

### How It Works

Quickly process text content with support for polish, expand, compress, and tone change.

### Usage Steps

1. **Select Text**
   - Select text in editor
   - Can be a sentence or paragraph

2. **Choose Operation**
   - Toolbar appears automatically
   - Select rewrite type:
     - Polish
     - Expand
     - Compress
     - Change Tone

3. **View Result**
   - AI displays result after processing
   - Can choose to apply or regenerate

4. **Apply Rewrite**
   - Click "Apply" to replace original
   - Or click "Cancel" to discard

### Rewrite Types

#### 1. Polish

Optimize text expression and improve quality.

**Use Cases:**
- Expression not smooth enough
- Word choice not accurate
- Want to improve writing

**Example:**

**Original:**
> "This method is very useful, I think everyone should try it."

**Polished:**
> "This method has been proven effective through practice, and I highly recommend everyone give it a try."

#### 2. Expand

Add content details and enrich expression.

**Use Cases:**
- Content too brief
- Need to add details
- Want fuller expression

**Example:**

**Original:**
> "Time management is important."

**Expanded:**
> "Time management is crucial for modern professionals. Good time management not only improves work efficiency but also helps us maintain clarity in busy work and achieve work-life balance. Mastering time management skills means taking control of your life."

#### 3. Compress

Simplify content and extract core points.

**Use Cases:**
- Content too lengthy
- Need concise expression
- Word count limitations

**Example:**

**Original:**
> "In today's fast-paced society, time management has become a very important skill. Whether at work or in life, good time management can help us complete tasks better, improve efficiency, and reduce stress."

**Compressed:**
> "Time management is key to improving efficiency and reducing stress."

#### 4. Change Tone

Modify expression style and tone.

**Supported Tone Types:**
- **Formal ‚Üí Casual** - Suitable for social media
- **Casual ‚Üí Formal** - Suitable for business
- **Objective ‚Üí Subjective** - Add personal opinion
- **Serious ‚Üí Humorous** - Add interest

## üí° Usage Tips

### Improve AI Quality

**1. Provide Sufficient Context**
- Detailed requirement description
- Specify target audience
- Indicate article style

**2. Use Specific Prompts**
```
Good prompt:
"Write a Python beginner's tutorial for zero-experience readers,
including environment setup, basic syntax, and simple examples,
with a casual and easy-to-understand style, with code examples
for each concept"

Bad prompt:
"Write Python tutorial"
```

**3. Iterate and Optimize**
- First generation may not be perfect
- Adjust prompts based on results
- Try multiple times for best results

**4. Combine with Manual Editing**
- AI generation as foundation
- Manual review and optimization
- Add personal opinions and examples

### Save API Costs

**1. Choose Cost-Effective Providers**
- DeepSeek: $0.14/1M tokens
- Zhipu AI: $0.014/1M tokens (GLM-4-Flash)

**2. Optimize Input Content**
- Avoid duplicate content
- Simplify unnecessary descriptions
- Use cheaper models for drafts

**3. Use Features Wisely**
- Title generation: Use GPT-3.5
- Article generation: Use DeepSeek
- Simple rewriting: Use GLM-4-Flash
