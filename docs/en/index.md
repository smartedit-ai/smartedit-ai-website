## Why Choose SmartEdit AI?

SmartEdit AI is a Chrome browser extension designed specifically for **WeChat Official Account creators**. It provides rich style templates, AI intelligent writing, one-click formatting, and image search through a sidebar interface, helping creators improve content creation efficiency and article quality.

### ğŸ¯ Core Advantages

- **Seamless Integration** - Directly embedded in WeChat Official Account editor, no page switching needed
- **AI Empowered** - Multiple AI service providers supported for intelligent high-quality content generation
- **Rich Styles** - 10+ curated style templates to meet various formatting needs
- **Simple Operation** - Click to use, no complex configuration required
- **Completely Free** - Open source project under MIT license, free forever
